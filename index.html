Rhein Main Kannada Sangha eV

Your home for Kannada culture and community in Germany

About Us

Rhein Main Kannada Sangha eV (RMKS) is a socio-cultural organization formed in 2015 to represent the Kannada-speaking community from Karnataka, India, settled in the Frankfurt Rhein-Main region of Germany. We promote Kannada culture, language, and community bonding.

Activities

Our activities include cultural events showcasing Kannada art, music, and dance, sports events like Yoga sessions and Badminton tournaments, and social initiatives such as supporting regions in need during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Education

We offer a structured online learning program called "Kannada Kali" to teach children the Kannada alphabet and language, preserving our linguistic heritage.
